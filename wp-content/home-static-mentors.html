Maven Ventures Growth Labs incubator programs are based in Palo Alto and run 6 months, in which time you'll have successfully built and scaled your business, raised a Seed round and learned the cutting-edge viral marketing and consumer growth techniques to achieve hypergrowth.

Over the past 20 years, Jim Scheinman has been privileged and honored to be a founder, investor, executive and advisor with very talented entrepreneurs building amazing companies that have delighted millions of customers, changed the world in a positive way, and returned a nice profit to the investors.

Maven Ventures Growth Labs is an early stage consumer startup incubator and seed capital fund that advises and invests in amazing entrepreneurs looking to change the world.
